Frequently Asked Questions

How do I buy tickets on Eventicko?

+

To buy tickets, simply browse our events, select the event you're interested in, choose your desired ticket quantity, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to provide your payment details to complete the purchase. Once confirmed, your tickets will be sent to your registered email address.

What payment methods does Eventicko accept?

+

We accept major credit and debit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, and American Express. We may also offer other local payment options depending on your region.

Can I get a refund for my ticket?

+

Refund policies vary depending on the event organizer. Please refer to the specific event's terms and conditions on its detail page. In general, all sales are final unless an event is canceled or significantly rescheduled.

What happens if an event is canceled or postponed?

+

If an event is canceled, you will typically receive a full refund automatically. If an event is postponed, your tickets will generally be valid for the new date. We will notify you via email with details on any cancellations or postponements and your options.

How do I access my tickets after purchase?

+

After a successful purchase, your tickets will be sent to the email address you provided during checkout. They may be attached as a PDF or link to a mobile ticket. Please check your spam or junk folder if you don't receive them within a few minutes. You can also typically access your tickets from your Eventicko account dashboard.

Do I need to print my tickets?

+

Many events accept mobile tickets displayed on your smartphone. However, it's always a good idea to check the specific event's requirements. If in doubt, printing a physical copy can serve as a backup.
